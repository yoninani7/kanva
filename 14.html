About MultiService Hub Italia

We are a premier service destination in Milan, combining convenience with sophistication to deliver exceptional experiences for both residents and visitors.

Elegant Solutions for Modern Living

Founded in 2018, MultiService Hub Italia was born from a simple idea: to create a single destination where people could access all essential services with premium quality and efficiency.

Our Milan location serves as a hub for the international community, offering everything from practical daily services to specialized offerings like traditional Ethiopian spices.

Authentic Ethiopian & Eritrean Spices

Taste of East Africa in Milan

Our spice collection is sourced directly from Ethiopia and Eritrea, ensuring authenticity and quality. From traditional Berbere to specialty coffee beans, we bring the flavors of East Africa to Italy.

  • Berbere Spice

    Traditional Ethiopian spice blend with chili peppers, garlic, ginger

  • Mitmita Powder

    Hot chili powder with cardamom, cloves, and salt

  • Nigella Seeds

    Black cumin with aromatic, slightly bitter flavor

  • Koseret Leaves

    Ethiopian oregano with citrusy, minty notes

  • Teff Flour

    Gluten-free flour for authentic Injera bread

  • Coffee Beans

    Premium Ethiopian Yirgacheffe and Sidamo beans
