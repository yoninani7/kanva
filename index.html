Knowledge Base

Essential Inquiries.

What identification is required for logistics pickup?

For absolute security, we strictly require a valid, government-issued photo ID (Passport, ID, or National Driver's License) that matches the recipient name on the DHL, FedEx, or Amazon package exactly. Digital copies are not accepted.

Do I need to book the workspace pods in advance?

While walk-ins are welcome, We strongly recommend reserving your slot via our WhatsApp concierge in advance to guarantee your quiet workspace.

Are the luggage vaults completely secure?

Yes. The Marula Vault is climate-controlled, protected by 24/7 CCTV surveillance, and features restricted physical access.

How does the corporate event atelier work?

Our Atelier team handles everything from venue scouting and high-end restaurant decor to full-scale corporate gala structuring. Contact our concierge to schedule a design consultation with our lead event architects.
