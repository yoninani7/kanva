Authentic Ethiopian & Eritrean Spices

Discover the rich flavors of East Africa with our carefully selected traditional spices and ingredients. Sourced directly from Ethiopia and Eritrea, our spices bring authentic taste to your kitchen.

  • Berbere - Traditional spice blend with chili peppers, garlic, ginger, and herbs
  • Mitmita - Hot chili powder with cardamom, cloves, and salt
  • Nigella Seeds - Black cumin with aromatic, slightly bitter flavor
  • Koseret - Ethiopian oregano with citrusy, minty notes
  • Teff Flour - Gluten-free flour for authentic Injera bread
  • Coffee Beans - Premium Ethiopian Yirgacheffe and Sidamo beans
